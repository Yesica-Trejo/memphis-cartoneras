About Cartoneras

Memphis Cartonera is a cooperative publishing house dedicated to democratize literature and art, to foster literacy, to work in sustainable ways, and to rethink the place of the humanities in the world. Memphis Cartoneras celebrates handmade books created from recycled cardboard, blending literature, visual art, and community activism.
